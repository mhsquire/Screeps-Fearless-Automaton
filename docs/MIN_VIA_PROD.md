# Minimum Viable Product

## Colony based behaviors

0. Spawn creeps
1. Mine things
2. Haul
3. Upgrade the Room

### Spawn Behavior

Spawn a hauler
Spawn a miner

### Mining Behavior

Walk toward Mine
Mine and drop, til death

### mining body

1 part move and 2 parts mine

### Hauling / Upgrading behavior

Walk toward Mine
Pickup til full

if spawn is priority
  Fill Spawn
else
  Fill controller
walk to mine

### Hauling Body

1 part move to 1 part carry
